// 01 ABOUT US

Built by Students,
For Students

Nexora is more than a club — it's a community where every B.Tech student, regardless of branch, finds their space to innovate, create, and connect.

We believe the best ideas emerge at the intersection of disciplines. That's why we host both cutting-edge tech events and vibrant non-tech activities — because a great engineer is also a great thinker.

⚡

Tech + Non-Tech Blend

Hackathons, coding contests, debates, art, and quizzes — all under one roof.

🚀

Placement Ready Resources

🌐

All Branches Welcome
